Olivetti Lettera 22 recording excerpts

The five hammer WAV files are processed excerpts of the 1:46 MP3 field
recording "typewriter-olivetti-lettra-22" by keithpeter (Freesound), served by
Pixabay's freesound_community account:

https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/typewriter-olivetti-lettra-22-20217/

Downloaded 2026-07-02 under the Pixabay Content License. The legally binding
terms and the human-readable summary are available at:

https://pixabay.com/service/terms/
https://pixabay.com/service/license-summary/

The strikes were isolated, trimmed to 140 ms, faded, filtered, compressed,
peak-normalised and encoded as mono PCM16 at 44.1 kHz. They are distributed as
integrated components of ayfor/typewriter packages, not as a standalone copy
or substitute for the source recording. Attribution is not required by the
licence, but is retained here as a permanent provenance record.

These are not Olympia Splendid 66 recordings. This limitation is why the
package is marked `fidelity: inspired`; measured Splendid 66 audio would
require a new version.
